Annual Competitions

SOTY & AOTY Selection Team

The SOTY and AOTY Selection Team is responsible for running the Hub's flagship annual competitions (Story of the Year and Author of the Year) from October through December each season.

Unlike the permanent teams, the SOTY and AOTY Selection Team is assembled fresh each year. Its composition changes to bring in new perspectives, ensure impartiality, and reflect the community's current landscape. Xavier leads the team every season.

If you're interested in serving as a judge in a future season, follow us on Instagram to find out when applications open.

01
Rebuilt every season The team composition changes year to year; new judges, fresh perspectives.
02
Led by Xavier Xavier heads the Selection Team every season alongside invited judges.
03
October – December The competition window runs for three months, concluding with winner announcements in late December.
04
60 / 40 scoring split Judge panel scores carry 60% of every story's result. Community votes account for the remaining 40%.

Want to be part of the team?

We recruit on an as-needed basis throughout the year. When a position opens, it's announced on our Instagram page. All roles are open to anyone who meets the age and qualification requirements.
