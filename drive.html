Review Drive

Every completed story review by the Buddies Review Team is stored here, organised by genre and accessible to anyone. Authors can read their own reviews, and the community can explore the feedback and criticism shared across every story we've assessed.

The Review Drive is open to everyone; authors, readers, and the wider Episode community. Use it to understand what the team looks for, learn from the feedback given to others, and see the standard of storytelling that earns a high score.
