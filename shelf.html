What is the EB Shelf?
The EB Shelf features the top five stories from our most recent monthly contest; winner, runner-up, and placements 3 through 5. It refreshes every time a new contest concludes.
02
How are stories named?
Each shelf is named using the contest's initials. Love In The Air becomes the LITA Shelf. The shelf name carries the contest identity and refreshes with each new round.
03
Want your story here?
Enter one of our monthly contests via the Instagram nomination post. Score well enough to place in the Top 5 and your story earns a spot on the next shelf.

How to land on the next shelf.

Every contest is a new shelf waiting to be filled. Here's how stories earn their spot.

01
Follow our Instagram All contest nomination calls are posted on @episode_buddies. That's where it starts.
02
Nominate or get nominated Comment your story on the nomination post within the 24-hour window. Stories must have 250k views or fewer.
03
Make the Top 10 The 10 most-nominated eligible stories go to the judging round. Score well enough to land in the Top 5.
04
Your story earns the shelf Top 5 stories are featured right here until the next contest concludes and a new shelf takes over.
