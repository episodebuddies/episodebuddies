What We Offer

Free services, built for Episode authors.

Request any of our services through the links below. Our team responds within 48 hours.

Service 01

Story Shoutout

We promote your story across our Instagram platform to help you reach new readers and grow your audience. A shoutout means your story gets featured directly to our followers with a call to action to read it.

We read a minimum of three chapters of your story before posting.
Six screenshots are captured; two per chapter and shared on our feed.
All shoutout content is reviewed and approved before posting.
Open to any Episode story that meets our basic eligibility criteria.

Story Review

Our review team provides honest, constructive feedback on your story; covering plot, directing, characters, writing style, and overall merit. A score of 90 or above automatically qualifies your story for the SOTY competition.

We review the first three episodes of your story only.

Service 03

Monthly Contests

We run one themed contest every month from February through December. The community nominates stories, the top ten are selected and judged, and winners are announced 2–3 weeks after nominations close.

Stories must have lesser than or exactly 250,000 views to be eligible.
Nominations are open for 24 hours via our Instagram post.
Top 10 most-nominated eligible stories proceed to judging.
Score 90+ in any contest to qualify for SOTY automatically.
