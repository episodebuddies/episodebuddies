How It Works

How to Enter

Entry is community-driven and completely free. Here's how the process works from start to finish.

01

Follow Our Instagram

All contest announcements are posted on @episode_buddies. That's where every nomination call goes up. Turn on notifications so you don't miss the window.

02

Solicit Nominations

Authors do not enter directly. Ask your readers, followers, or community to nominate your story in the comments of our Instagram nomination post. The more nominations, the better your chances.

03

Make the Top 10

The 10 most-nominated eligible stories go forward to the judging round. Our team evaluates each one using our full judging rubric.

04

Results Are Announced

Winners are revealed on our Instagram. The top 5 stories earn a spot on the EB Shelf and the winner qualifies automatically for SOTY 2026.

Rules & Eligibility

What makes a story eligible?

We keep our rules straightforward. Read through each one before nominating or asking someone to nominate your story. If you have a question not covered here, send us a message.

Ask Us on Instagram
Stories must have fewer than or equal to 250,000 story views to be eligible. Stories above this threshold will not be accepted into the contest.
Stories that have entered any Buddies contest within the last 12 months are not eligible. This keeps the competition fresh and gives new stories their moment.
Stories that have already been featured on the EB Shelf are not eligible to enter again. This ensures our spotlight reaches as many new stories as possible.
Nominations are only open for 24 hours after the nomination post goes live on Instagram. Any nominations submitted after the window closes will not be counted.
For contests without a specified genre restriction, stories from any genre are welcome to enter. Themed contests (like Her Story, Her Power) may have additional creative guidance.
Your story must have a minimum of three published or available episodes at the time of nomination. Stories with only one or two episodes will not be accepted.
Stories that have not been updated in more than one year are not eligible, unless the story is marked as completed. Completed stories are always welcome regardless of last update date.
Judging Criteria

What we judge on.

Every entry is reviewed against the same criteria by our judging team. Here's exactly what we look for.

01

First Impression

How does the story open? Does the cover, title, and opening chapter pull you in immediately?

02

Theme Fit

For themed contests, how well does the story embody and explore the contest theme?

03

Directing

The quality of scene composition, camera use, overlays, and overall visual storytelling.

04

Plot

How engaging, original, and well-structured is the story's narrative arc and pacing?

05

Interactivity

How meaningfully does the story incorporate reader choices and decision points?

+

Additional Criteria

Grammar & writing quality · Use of sound and music · Diversity & representation · Character development
