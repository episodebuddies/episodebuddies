01
What is the Buddies Library?
A curated, permanent collection of the best stories to pass through the Buddies Support Hub. Every story here has earned its place through competition; not by popularity, but by quality.
02
What stories qualify?
The top 4 from last year's SOTY competition, plus the top 3 winners from each of the last 5 monthly contests. The library is updated every year after the SOTY season closes.
03
How do I get in?
Reach the Top 4 in SOTY, or win one of the monthly contests. The library is a reflection of excellence; earn your spot through the competition, and you'll be here permanently.
