About the Hub

Built to support every Episode author.

Buddies Support Hub was founded in August 2022 with a simple belief; that quality storytelling deserves recognition, no matter the author's following or experience level.

We offer free services, run a structured season of monthly contests, and award a Story of the Year and Author of the Year each December. Everything we do is for the community.
