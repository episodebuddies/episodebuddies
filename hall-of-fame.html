Every Author of the Year.

AOTY recognizes the full author; their craft, presence, communication, and community impact. The title is held for life.

◆ 2025 Author of the Year ◆
2025 AOTY Winner
Author of the Year 2025
R. Lewis
The Clique delivers a gripping narrative where contrasting worlds collide and navigate the chaos of life together. With outstanding, movie-style directing and immersive storytelling, R. Lewis captivates readers and leaves a powerful, lasting impression.
1st Runner Up
1st Runner Up · AOTY 2025
Charlie Seb
Charlie Seb's debut story Only Pretend is a powerful mix of hope, second chances, and raw emotion. With heartfelt storytelling and unforgettable character journeys, it has captured hearts worldwide.
2nd Runner Up
2nd Runner Up · AOTY 2025
Tali
With Prelude To Forever, Tali crafts an enchanting and mysterious narrative, drawing readers into a story rich in emotion, tension, and timeless intrigue.
3rd Runner Up
3rd Runner Up · AOTY 2025
Nas
Through One Latte At A Time, Nas beautifully captures the essence of love in its purest form. With refined writing and emotional depth, her storytelling resonates deeply.
◆ 2024 Author of the Year ◆
2024 AOTY Winner
Author of the Year 2024
Ryan Graye
Ryan Graye's debut story Polaroids has taken the Episode community by storm. With its heartfelt narrative and captivating characters, it's clear she has made an unforgettable entrance as an author.
1st Runner Up
1st Runner Up · AOTY 2024
Layvi
Through Fire and the Flood, Layvi masterfully portrayed love and resilience in the LGBTQ+ genre. Her storytelling brilliance has resonated deeply with readers, making it a true masterpiece.
2nd Runner Up
2nd Runner Up · AOTY 2024
Natalie Xo
With heartfelt tales like The Hitman's Dilemma, Natalie has shown incredible versatility and depth as an author. Her ability to tell emotionally rich stories is truly remarkable.
3rd Runner Up
3rd Runner Up · AOTY 2024
Nalia Elnira
Nalia's 'The Dove' touches the soul, offering readers hope and the beauty of healing. Her talent for weaving such an impactful tale has left an everlasting impression on the community.
◆ 2023 Author of the Year ◆

After publishing her first story 'Inventions of C. Christopher', Fern anticipated the success that would come with crafting Symphony. Her continued growth has propelled her to new heights where her talent will always shine brightly.
